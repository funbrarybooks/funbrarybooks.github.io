Paris

By
Marco Bear Paris! The first thing you do when you get to Paris, France, is settle in and make yourself at home in this beautiful city. As you stroll through the cobble stone streets, relax and enjoy everything! The scenery, the croissants, the people and the food! Me, Khaki and Dingue took a picture outside of the Lourve museum which, from the outside, looks like a giant glass pyramid. The art inside the museum was unbelievable! You have to go see the Mona Lisa there. Afterwards we went to Coutume, a french cafe, to get a latte. It was delicious! In the morning the next day we went to see the Eiffel tower which was so beautiful and the Sacre-Coeur which is a great place to see a view of the city and enjoy hanging out with some friends. My favorite part of the trip was watching a group of men play bocce ball outside in the park. I would go back again and this time enjoy more food!
