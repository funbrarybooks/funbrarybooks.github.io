New York

By
Marco Bear I am so lucky I got to see New York City! I've heard about it from so many friends and so many movies are filmed there. It was not what I expected. It's impossible to see ALL the city has to offer in one trip. I was able to see somethings though! Just from the cab ride from the Airport to my friends house I saw downtown and people were moving so fast! We drove past the Public Library which is a beautiful building with lion statues on the outside. After me and Khaki settled in, we took a walk around. The blocks were long! I recommend you to visit Central Park! The park is huge and centered in the middle of the city, I walked around and enjoyed watching the ducks in the pond. I stopped and grabbed a street veggie hot dog, a classic thing to do in the city. New York is known for it's skyscrapers, so I had to check one out. Haha, Khaki and I went to the Empire State Building, from the observatory deck, we were able to see views of the whole city. It was incredible! New York is a big city and there was so much more to see!
