Thank You For Singning Up!

About Us

MARCOFounder

Hi, I’m Marco, a polar bear who loves reading. Unfortunately, there is no book written in Bear Language. So I decided to collet children books from different places and learn different languages. My dream is to build a fun place called “Funbrary” for children from all over the world, so they can learn to read, to speak new languages, to share their culture with one another and have fun! I have been traveling around the world and collected over 100 books, each of them is written in multiple languages.

Now, I invite you to join me to build our Funbrary, a library full of unexpected fun!

Feature

Beyond A Children’s Library Funbrary is not only a library that provides reading to children in their native language, but also a place where children can be entertained by Marco no matter where they are.

Contains Multiple Languages Funbrary is dedicated to provide cultural diversity to children. Each book in the Funbrary is written in multiple languages and translated by highly qualified translators.

It’s Animated Funbrary offers unique and animated children books. With vivid animation, books are audiovisual, which create an easier reading and learning experience.

It’s Flexible Funbrary books provide two user-friendly reading methods: 1.”Read to me” activates the selected language, and the story is read by a native. 2. ”Read by myself” helps children to focus on the vocabulary and spelling.

Kids’ Gallery Funbrary encourages children to express themselves. In kids’ gallery, We showcase their stories and drawings, Help children to share and connect with the world better.

Caring Design Funbrary is founded by a group of children-loving artists. Our App is children friendly and easy to use. Through the carefully selected titles, children will learn positive and dynamic life experiences.
